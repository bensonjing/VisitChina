CHONGQING

Chongqing City is regarded as “Mountain City”, located in the southeastern part of the Sichuan Basin and the junction of the Yangtze River and Jialing River. It was the only municipality directly under the central government in China's western region. Owing to its strong comprehensive strength in science and technology and a solid foundation for scientific and technological industry, Chongqing becomes a great power in the Western region and a base of technology on the upper reaches of the Yangtze River.
